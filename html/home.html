Managing Diabetes means managing data. Every day we are faced with individual numbers that make up a bigger, and more important picture. Without seeing trends in the data, how are we supposed to learn from it?

Line is a platform to visualize, contextualize and share blood sugar data. We'll be rolling out early versions of our product over the next couple of months. If you give us your email, we'll let you know when we are ready for you. Your involvment is what we need to make a product that will change people's lives.
