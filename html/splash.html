Connecting the dots

In healthcare we are often referred to as patients or consumers, we believe that more than anything we are producers of our own health. But in order best produce our health we need to be able to connect to ourselves, the people we care about, communities of people like us, and our healthcare providers. We believe these connections are broken, and we want to fix them. We want to connect the dots.
