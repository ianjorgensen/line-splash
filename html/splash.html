Connecting the dots for people with diabetes.

Managing Diabetes means managing data. Every day we are faced with individual numbers that make up a bigger, and more important picture. Without seeing trends in the data, how are we supposed to learn from it?

Line is a platform to visualize, contextualize and share blood sugar data. We'll be rolling out early versions of our product over the next couple of months.
